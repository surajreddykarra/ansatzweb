Access and privacy

Read-only by design.

What PostRun needs

OAuth access to Garmin and Strava with read permissions only, so the app can fetch run stats.

What PostRun does not intend to do

We do not intend to access, read, or store user data for anything unrelated to customizing run stats for sharing.

Why this exists

The product is focused on helping runners personalize the way their runs appear on social media after activity sync completes.
